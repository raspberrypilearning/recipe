香蕉奶昔

原料：

  • 1个香蕉
  • 250毫升牛奶
  • 3 ice cubes
  • blender

Method:

  1. Peel the banana and add to the blender
  2. Add the milk and ice cubes to the blender
  3. Turn on the blender for 30 seconds
  4. Drink!
